Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Court Vision is an NBA college quiz game created by Anthony. This Privacy Policy explains what information the site collects, how it is used, and what choices you have when you use the website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use Court Vision:

  • Account information you choose to provide, such as your username and Google sign-in details.
  • Gameplay information, such as scores, ranked Elo, match history, friend requests, and online status.
  • Technical information, such as basic browser, device, usage, and analytics data used to keep the site working.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • run the game and maintain your profile, rankings, friends list, and online matches
  • improve site performance, reliability, and mobile usability
  • monitor abuse, cheating, spam, and misuse of online features
  • understand traffic and site activity for product and monetization decisions

Google Sign-In

If you sign in with Google, Court Vision uses Google account information needed to verify your identity and associate your account with your saved profile. Your Google password is not stored by Court Vision.

Cookies And Similar Technologies

Court Vision may use local storage, session storage, and similar browser technologies to keep you signed in, remember settings, and improve the gameplay experience.

Analytics And Advertising

Court Vision may use analytics tools and, in the future, advertising services to understand traffic and help support the site. Those services may collect information according to their own policies.

Sharing Of Information

Court Vision does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared with infrastructure, authentication, analytics, or advertising providers only as needed to operate and improve the site.

Data Retention

Gameplay, account, and ranking data may be kept as long as reasonably necessary to operate Court Vision, maintain leaderboards, prevent abuse, and improve the service.

Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to sign in with Google.
  • You can stop using the site at any time.
  • You can contact Court Vision to request help with account-related questions.

Children's Privacy

Court Vision is not intended for children under 13, and the site is not knowingly directed to young children.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of Court Vision after updates means the revised policy will apply.

Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, or business inquiries, visit the Contact page.
